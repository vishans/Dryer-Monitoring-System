FAQ

Q: How does this work?

A: There are 3 limit switches around the dryer dial to detect its position. The system can thus determine if the machine is on or off.

Q: Is there an electrocution risk?

A: No. The system operates at 3.3V DC. As comparison, a modern smartphone operates at around 3.7V DC.

Q: What does the system monitor exactly?

A: The system can only tell when the dryer is on or off.
It does not know who is using the dryer at any time.

Q: How long does it take to detect the dryer status?

A: About 5 seconds.

Q: Where can I access this service?

A: This service only operates within the house's Wi-Fi network. If you're not connected to this network, you won't have access to it.

Q: When can I access this service?

A: I am running this on my PC. If and when I turn off this PC, the service will be interrupted.

Q: Will the link to this website ever change?

A: It is possible. I will let you know if ever the link has changed.

Q: What happened to the last version of the system?

A: Because the washer sometimes vibrates vehemently when in use, it would occasionally false trigger the vibration sensor. I am now using limit switches, which are far more reliable.

This service is still in beta.
